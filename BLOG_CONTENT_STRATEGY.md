## Article Specifications

### **Content Requirements**

- **Word Count**: 1,500-3,000 words (depending on topic complexity)
- **Tone**: Professional but friendly, Australian English (colour, organisation, etc.)
- **Target Audience**: Australian job seekers (graduates, career changers, professionals)
- **Reading Level**: Grade 8-10 (easy to understand)

### **Structure for Each Article**

1. **H1 Title** (include target keyword)
2. **Introduction** (150-200 words, hook + problem statement)
3. **Table of Contents** (for articles >2,000 words)
4. **H2 Sections** (3-7 sections with H3 subsections)
5. **Actionable Tips** (numbered lists, bullet points, examples)
6. **Conclusion** (100-150 words, summary + CTA)
7. **FAQ Section** (3-5 common questions)

### **SEO Requirements**

- **Primary Keyword**: Use in H1, first paragraph, one H2, URL slug
- **Secondary Keywords**: 3-5 related keywords sprinkled naturally
- **Internal Links**: Link to Career Hub AI tools (Resume Builder, ATS Checker)
- **External Links**: 2-3 authoritative sources (government job sites, LinkedIn research)
- **Meta Description**: 150-160 characters, include primary keyword
- **Images**: 3-5 custom images or screenshots per article
- **Alt Text**: Descriptive alt text for all images

### **Call-to-Action (CTA) Placement**

Every article must include:

1. **Mid-article CTA**: "Try Career Hub AI's free resume builder →"
2. **End-article CTA**: "Start building your ATS-optimized resume in 10 minutes"
3. **Sidebar CTA**: "Free Resume Templates" (link to Resume Builder)
